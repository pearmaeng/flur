VIVID VEINS is an alternative pop band that draws inspiration from the vibrant beauty of nature and the emotional depth of human connection. The band's name reflects their passion for bringing life and energy to their music, much like the veins of a leaf that carry vitality. Their debut album, FLUR, intertwines the theme of flowers with their signature vivid sound, symbolizing growth, resilience, and the harmony of life. Through bold visuals and heartfelt melodies, VIVID VEINS invites listeners on a journey of self-discovery and emotional exploration, celebrating the beauty found in both strength and fragility.
